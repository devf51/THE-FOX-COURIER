# Music Download Guide

## Quick Download Steps

### Track 1: Ambient Horror (CC0) ⭐ RECOMMENDED
1. Visit: https://opengameart.org/content/ambient-horror
2. Click "ambient_horror.ogg" download link
3. Save as: `ambient_horror.ogg`

### Track 2: Horror Ambient (CC-BY 3.0)
1. Visit: https://opengameart.org/content/horror-ambient
2. Click "horror ambient.ogg" download link  
3. Save as: `horror_ambient.ogg`

### Track 3: Scary Ambient Wind (CC-BY 3.0)
1. Visit: https://opengameart.org/content/scary-ambient-wind
2. Click "Scary Ambient Wind.ogg" download link
3. Save as: `scary_ambient_wind.ogg`

### Track 4: Dark Cavern Ambient Loop (CC0) ⭐ RECOMMENDED
1. Visit: https://opengameart.org/content/dark-cavern-ambient
2. Click "dark_cavern_ambient_002.ogg" (the loop version)
3. Save as: `dark_cavern_ambient.ogg`

### Track 5: Search Freesound.org
1. Visit: https://freesound.org
2. Create free account (if needed)
3. Search: "dark ambient horror loop"
4. Filter by .ogg format and CC0 or CC-BY license
5. Download your preferred track
